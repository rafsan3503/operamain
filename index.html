WHAT IS
OPERA PROTOCOL?

OPERA Protocol is a new DAO Governed Liquidity Bank specifically for the purpose of launching new tokens across several blockchains in a safe a permissionless way.

OPERA works by allowing developers, builders, and degens to “get liquid” and fund their LPs (Liquidity Pools) with ETH, without having to ask anyone or wait for anything.

$OPERA holders can choose to lend ETH, BNB, or other tokens to provide liquidity to new tokens and earn a passive yield virtually risk free.


WHY USE
OPERA?

The Opera Protocol itself primarily serves 3 groups of people.

1. Borrowers that want to launch tokens without the hassle of providing and locking personal funds for liquidity.

2. Lenders which want to lend their tokens/funds to those borrowers to be used as liquidity.

3. Lastly, there will be users that just want to participate in the ecosystem, via holding, voting, etc.

Opera Protocol is the gate to be a part of something that is helping developers get liquid every single day. Anyone can participate on any side, supply or demand.
